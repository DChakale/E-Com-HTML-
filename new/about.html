About Online Shoping

Online Shoping is a form of electronic commerce which allows consumers to directly buy goods or services from a seller over the internet using a web browser. Consumer find a product of interest by using the website of the retailer directly or by serching among alternative vendors using a shoping search engine,whch displays the same product avilibility and pricing at different e-retailler. As of 2022 customer can stop online using a range of different computers and divices including desktops computers,laptops,tablet computers,smartphone and smart spekers.

An online shoping evokes the physical anology of buying products or services at a reguler "bricks-and-mortar"retailler or shopping center;the process is called business-to consumer (B2C) online shopping.When an online store is set up to enable business to buy form another business,the process is called business-to-business (B2B) online shopping. A typical online store enables the customer to browse the firm's range of products and services,view photos or images of the products,along with information about the product specification,fetures and prices.
